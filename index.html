Something about my skills

Born in Mumbai(my mother's native place), but my childhood memories still lies in Kolhapur(my father's native place), where I did my schooling and rocked with my cousins playing cricket and other games enjoying the innocence of village life. Not sure what inspired me to the way of art but I still remember my father buying colors for me along with fantasy tales having vibrant toon images.

When the heart blooms your lips widen with the cheerfulness of life and thats what the colors did to me. I was so passionate about the way pencils drew things on my book and the colors carved the impressions on my mind. Appreciations help you build confidence and hence I cherish the support given by my parents, friends and well-wishes throughout my childhood which helped me to grow my art skills.

Realizing the truth that my thirst for destiny has not settled, my curiosity and enthusiasm triggers my blood to explore and learn more into the world of art. Technology, the signature of the era, has now been the way of living for me, expressing my art skills in digital forms, huh.. so far so good!
